Fischer

Fischer Time consists of a basic main time and each time a player makes a move the Bonus is added to it. The player loses once his clock reaches 0.

Byoyomi

Byoyomi consists of a basic main time and byoyomi periods. Once the main time is over, the player starts overtime and he has to play within the byoyomi time. If he misses the time, he will lose a period. The player loses after his clock reaches 0 and he has no more periods.

Hourglass

In Hourglass each second a player takes is added to his opponent time. A player loses after the difference between his clock and his opponents exceeds the differential time. Kaya.gs has a slight modification: if you play, you will at least have 10 seconds to think your next move.

Absolute

Absolute time only has a fix time for each player. The player loses once his clock reaches 0.

None

There is no clock running. The game can take as long as the players want to.
